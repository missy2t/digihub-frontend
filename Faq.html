General Questions

What is Digi Hub?

Digi Hub is a digital marketplace where students can buy and access study materials securely.

Who can use Digi Hub?

Digi Hub is designed for students looking for reliable academic resources. Anyone with an account can browse, buy, and use study materials.

Buying & Accessing Materials

How do I purchase study materials?

Simply browse by department and course, select a material, and click the "Buy" button to complete the purchase.

How do I access materials after purchase?

Once purchased, the study material will be available for download in your account.

Payments & Security

What payment methods are supported?

Digi Hub supports Ethiopian payment gateways like Chapa for secure transactions.

Is my payment information safe?

Yes. We use secure encryption and trusted payment gateways to ensure your financial details remain safe.

Content & Intellectual Property

Are the materials on Digi Hub verified?

We implement content protection measures to ensure the quality and reliability of uploaded study materials.

Can I share purchased materials with others?

No. Materials are protected with watermarking and download restrictions to prevent unauthorized sharing.

Support

What if I have an issue with my purchase?

If you experience any issues, contact our support team through the "Contact" page.
